Cockatiel

The cockatiel also known as the weero/weiro or quarrion is a medium-sized parrot that is a member of its own branch of the cockatoo family endemic to Australia.The cockatiel is the only member of the genus Nymphicus

Cockatiel Favorite Things in Order!

  • 1. Play with movable parts on toys
  • 2. To chatter and whistle
  • 3. Like to be touched
